Homvana Air Purifier: Breathe Cleaner, Live Healthier

The Homvana Air Purifier is a top-tier solution designed for large living spaces like living rooms, open kitchens, lofts, and basements. With a powerful CADR of 142 CFM / 240 m3/h, it efficiently cleans up to 1250 square feet in just 60 minutes, ensuring fresh air throughout your home. This air purifier handles a variety of airborne impurities, including dust, pollen, smoke, pet dander, and more, making it a must-have for families seeking a healthier environment.

Advanced 4-Stage Filtration with H13 True HEPA

Built with a 4-stage filtration system, the Homvana Air Purifier includes a high-efficiency H13 True HEPA filter that captures 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.1 microns. This ensures cleaner air for individuals with allergies or asthma, providing a safer and more comfortable indoor atmosphere. The unit also features a real-time air quality indicator that changes color from green to red to alert you when air quality declines.

Smart Auto Mode & 22dB Quiet Operation

Experience effortless control with the Homvana Air Purifier's Smart Auto Mode, which automatically adjusts fan speed based on air quality. This feature eliminates the need for manual adjustments and ensures consistent performance. In addition, the 22dB SilentAir Tech provides ultra-quiet operation, perfect for late-night use or achieving restful sleep without interruption. You can also control the air purifier using the device panel or the Homvana App, offering convenience at your fingertips.
