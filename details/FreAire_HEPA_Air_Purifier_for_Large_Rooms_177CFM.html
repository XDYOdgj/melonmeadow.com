FreAire EPI266 HEPA Air Purifier

Achieve pristine indoor air with the FreAire EPI266, a high-performance air purifier engineered for spacious environments. Whether you're looking to improve air quality in your home, bedroom, living room, or a room with pets, this unit delivers a continuously clean and fresh atmosphere.

Efficient Air Purification

Engineered with a powerful CADR of 177CFM, the FreAire EPI266 ensures rapid purification of up to 1,420 square feet in just one hour. This makes it ideal for high-traffic areas and larger living spaces where clean air is essential.

Advanced Filtration System

Featuring an upgraded H13 HEPA filter, the FreAire EPI266 captures 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns. Dust, pollen, pet dander, and harmful microorganisms are effectively removed, contributing to a healthier and more comfortable indoor environment.

Real-Time Air Quality Monitoring

Equipped with a high-precision infrared PM2.5 sensor, this air purifier continuously tracks air quality and displays it in real time. The intuitive 4-color display (green, blue, yellow, red) provides instant insight into air conditions, letting you know when action is needed.

Smart and Quiet Operation

Experience advanced smart functionality with auto mode that automatically adjusts fan speed depending on air quality. For a peaceful night's sleep, the sleep mode delivers a quiet operation at just 22dB and disables the indicator light, ensuring a calm and restful environment.

Beyond being a HEPA air purifier, the FreAire EPI266 serves as an effective air quality monitor that enriches your everyday life with cleaner, more breathable air. Choose this model for large rooms to improve your health and comfort at home.

  • Upgraded H13 HEPA filter removes 99.97% of 0.3-micron particles
