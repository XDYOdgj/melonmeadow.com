Hunter HP400 HEPA Air Purifier: Breathe Easier in Every Room

A reliable and efficient solution for clean air in your home, office, or bedroom

The Hunter HP400 air purifier is designed to bring clean, fresh air into your living space. Its advanced True HEPA filter captures 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns, effectively removing dust, pollen, smoke, and allergens from the air. This ensures a healthier environment for you and your family, especially in sensitive areas like bedrooms or nurseries.

Enhancing air quality has never been easier with the Hunter HP400. The built-in EcoSilver pre-filter helps eliminate bacteria and viruses, while the coconut carbon pre-filter targets VOCs and pet odors. Its quiet operation, running at just 34 decibels, makes it ideal for use in sleep mode without disturbing your rest. With easy-to-read filter replacement indicators, maintenance is stress-free and simple.

With digital controls offering 3 fan speeds, a sleep mode, and a programmable timer, you can customize your air purification experience for maximum convenience and comfort.
